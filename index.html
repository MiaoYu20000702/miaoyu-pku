Welcome to my research homepage!
I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the National School of Development, Peking University, and a visiting PhD student at the University of Pennsylvania (2024.09–2025.07).
My research interests include labor economics, family economics, public economics and health economics, with a focus on the economic impacts of fertility, gender gaps in labor markets, and AI in labor markets.
